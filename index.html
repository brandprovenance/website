The Problem

Brand guidelines were
not built for how
work moves today.

The work of establishing and protecting a brand has always been complex. But something has changed. Creative processes now move through more tools, more agents, and more automated workflows than ever before.

Brand rules that live in static PDFs or internal wikis cannot travel through these systems with any reliability. Identity fragments. Consistency erodes. The brand becomes harder to steward, not because teams stopped caring, but because the infrastructure was never designed for this.

01

Brand guidelines are operationally weak, written for people, not for systems or AI-assisted workflows.

02

Identity rules scatter across documents, tools, and teams. No common format. No shared structure.

03

As processes grow more distributed, brand logic depends increasingly on human memory. That doesn't scale.

What We're Growing

Two things.
One direction.

BRND-standard and Brand Provenance are related but distinct. Here is how to think about them.

BRND-standard

The open agentic brand standard.

BRND-standard is an open specification for portable, machine-readable brand identity. It treats brand as infrastructure: structured, interoperable, and designed to travel across AI workflows, agentic systems, and distributed creative processes without losing coherence. Think of it as the mortar between the bricks, connecting your brand to many workflows rather than locking it inside one. Licensed under CC BY 4.0. Any organisation may implement it independently.

Brand Provenance

The public home of BRND-standard.

Brand Provenance is the website and public communication surface for BRND-standard, owned and operated by Girling Design. It is where BRND-standard is published, communicated, and made accessible to brands, agencies, developers, and organisations. It is not itself a separate product. It is the front door.

Early Stage

v0.1.0-alpha. In public. Growing carefully.

BRND-standard is currently in v0.1.0-alpha, founder-led, open for community comment, and sufficiently formed for early implementation and discussion. You no longer need to wait for the dust to settle. As AI models improve, your BRND-standard remains and only grows sharper. As new expansions are added, your brand identity becomes more portable, more interoperable, and more resilient. You move with distinction and certainty as the AI infrastructure around you evolves.
