The Problem

Brand guidelines were
not built for how
work moves today.

The work of establishing and protecting a brand has always been complex. But something has changed. Creative processes now move through more tools, more agents, and more automated workflows than ever before.

Brand rules that live in static PDFs or internal wikis cannot travel through these systems with any reliability. Identity fragments. Consistency erodes. The brand becomes harder to steward, not because teams stopped caring, but because the infrastructure was never designed for this.

01

Brand guidelines are operationally weak, written for people, not for systems or AI-assisted workflows.

02

Identity rules scatter across documents, tools, and teams. No common format. No shared structure.

03

As processes grow more distributed, brand logic depends increasingly on human memory. That doesn't scale.

What We're Growing

Two things.
One direction.

BRND-standard and Brand Provenance are related but distinct. Here is how to think about them.

BRND-standard

The open agentic brand standard.

BRND-standard is an open specification for portable, machine-readable brand identity. It treats brand as infrastructure: structured, interoperable, and designed to travel across AI workflows, agentic systems, and distributed creative processes without losing coherence. Think of it as the mortar between the bricks, connecting your brand to many workflows rather than locking it inside one. Licensed under CC BY 4.0. Any organisation may implement it independently.

Brand Provenance

The public home of BRND-standard.

Brand Provenance aptly is the originator of the BRND-standard. This website is where BRND-standard is published, communicated, and made accessible to brands, agencies, developers, and organisations. It is not itself a separate product. It is the front door.

In Practice

What BRND-standard
actually looks like.

A working demonstration using Aether Protocol: a minimum viable brand concept. The same brief, processed two ways.

Without BRND-standard

"It's not just a lattice, it's a revolutionary way to delve into your data. We utilize seamless transitions to orchestrate a tapestry of insights for our customers — making your workflow better."

  • Banned pattern: "It's not just X, it's Y"
  • Banned words: delve, tapestry, revolutionary
  • "Utilize" and "Customers" flagged as substitutions
  • Unspaced em-dash — incorrect for en-GB dialect
With BRND-standard applied

"Our lattice provides a precise way to use your data. We orchestrate insights for our members, building a foundation for their protocol."

  • Negation reframing removed
  • Lexical guardrails enforced throughout
  • Sentence restructured: no punctuation dependency
  • Tone aligned: visionary outlook without hype

The logic that produced this output — voice register, lexical guardrails, dialect rules, tone parameters — is defined once in structured markdown files and travels with the brand across any compliant system.

Tone System

Voice as a
governed parameter set.

Every voice expression in BRND-standard is defined by a three-axis coordinate, channel register × emotive mode × thematic purpose. Tone dials set the intensity within that coordinate. The same brand, two completely different expressions.

Voice Coordinate A
linkedin-post × restrained × thought-leadership
warmth
0.0 authority
+0.7 brevity
+0.5 energy
−0.3

"Brand rules written for people cannot travel through AI workflows reliably. That is not a technology limitation. It is a structural one. BRND-standard addresses the structure."

Voice Coordinate B
instagram-caption × celebratory × community-signal
warmth
+0.8 energy
+0.7 formality
−0.5 brevity
+0.3

"Two years of building this in public. Thank you to everyone who came early and stayed curious. The work is sharper because you were here."

Same brand. Same dial defaults. Different coordinate, different expression instance. The dials are the stochastic layer that keeps variation bounded and characteristically consistent across every output.

Visual Logic

The same logic,
applied to image-making.

Photographic parameters follow the same dial structure as voice, governing subject presence, light character, expression range, depth, and colour temperature. A different configuration produces a categorically different image brief, from the same brand.

Visual Configuration A
editorial · measured authority
subject_presence
−0.6 expression_range
−0.4 light_quality
−0.3 depth_of_field
−0.5 colour_temperature
−0.2

Mid-distance frame. Subject composed, direct to camera. Clean, flat light with directional control. Sharp field throughout. Cool, controlled palette.

Visual Configuration B
intimate · brand warmth
subject_presence
+0.7 expression_range
+0.6 light_quality
+0.7 depth_of_field
+0.6 colour_temperature
+0.5

Close frame with selective focus. Subject relaxed, present, not performing. Warm, side-lit atmosphere. Compressed depth, soft background separation. Warm, saturated palette.

The same numerical logic that governs voice governs image-making. Within this framework, a BRND-compliant visual brief is as structured, portable, and machine-readable as a voice coordinate, ensuring any compliant system can interpret both with equal guidance. These examples illustrate the broader concept of codifying brand components for agentic utility and are not intended as literal specifications.

 

While voice and image are presented here as two codified domains for illustrative purposes, they represent a broader interoperability for assets built to be fit-for-purpose. Ultimately, the BRND-standard functions not as an agent itself, but as a universal, preferred terminology that allows AI agents to build within a unified structural future.

How It Works

Three steps to
portable brand logic.

01

Structure your brand logic

Define your brand's voice, tone system, visual rules, positioning, and dialect behaviour in structured markdown files, one concern per file, organised by the BRND-standard specification.

02

Build your BRND-compliant repository

Your brand identity lives in a governed, versioned repository, structured for machine readability as well as human use. The standard defines the architecture. You own the content.

03

Plug into compliant systems

Any AI tool, agentic workflow, or creative system built to read BRND-standard gets your brand logic reliably, not a summary, not an approximation. The governed source.
